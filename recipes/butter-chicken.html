Butter Chicken

Description:

An affordable recipe for Butter Chicken curry from Joshua Weissman of Social Media fame.

Ingredients:

Chicken:

  • 1 cup (240) full-fat yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp (15g) garam masala
  • 1 Tbsp (15g) kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Butter Chicken Curry:

  • 2 Tbsp (30g) vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2-inch knob of ginger, finely diced
  • 6 cloves of sliced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp (8g) paprika
  • 2 tsp (9g) ground cumin
  • 1 Tbsp (14g) garam masala
  • 1 1/2 tsp (6g) turmeric powder *optional
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup (160ml) water
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 2 Tbsp (28g) unsalted butter
  • Steamed rice
  • Fresh cilantro *optional

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken thighs with yogurt, garam masala, and salt for 30 minutes or overnight
  2. Heat vegetable oil in large pan over medium-high heat until shimmering hot.
  3. Add chicken and sear each side 2-3 minutes
  4. Remove chicken from pan, lower to medium, add a bit more oil with diced onions, diced ginger, sliced garlic, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix and stir, sauteing for 2-3 minutes or till softened.
  5. Add spices and stil till toasted for 1-2 minutes
  6. Add crushed tomatoes and water, stir until incorporated over medium heat. Simmer 5-8 minutes until sauce is reduced.
  7. Add back chicken, mix and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Add heavy cream, mix and bring to light simmer before lowering heat to low, simmer for no longer than 3-4 minutes. Stir often.
  9. Turn off heat and stir unsalted butter in till melted, season to taste with salt.
  10. Plate with rice and top with cilantro.
