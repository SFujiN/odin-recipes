Yuru Camp Sukiyaki

Description

The Famous, one and only, delicious(TBD), Christmas Sukiyaki dish from Yuru Camp S1E11!!!

Ingredients:

Sukiyaki:

  • 1 lb beef(chuck or ribeye)
  • 1 Tbsp beef fat
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup sake
  • shiitake and enoki mushrooms
  • green onions(negi)
  • tofu
  • noodles
  • chrysanthemum greens(spinach)
  • eggs

Tomato Sukiyaki:

Steps:

Sukiyaki:

  1. Thinly slice beef.
  2. Coat pot with beef fat.
  3. Lightly fry beef in pot.
  4. Add sugar, soy sauce, and sake, then bring to a simmer
  5. Add tofu, noodles, and greens. Bring pot to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve in bowls, crack egg in seperate bowl for dipping, and enjoy!
