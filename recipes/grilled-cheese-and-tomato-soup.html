Grilled Cheese & Tomato Soup

Description:

An ole American classic dish. Apparently first made by a women named Lorena Howell in the 1920s. Recipe inspired by Adam Ragusea.

Ingredients:

Tomato Soup:

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 fennel bulb (optional)
  • 1 stick 4oz (113g) butter (or 1/4 of measurements)
  • pepper
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 cup (30g) flour
  • 2, 28 oz (880g) cans of tomatoes
  • 1 cup (237mL) white wine (optional)
  • salt
  • water
  • sugar (optional, if not sweet enough)
  • tomato paste (optional, for more tomatoe taste)
  • olive oil (optional, for garnish)
  • chili flakes (optional, for garnish)
  • garlic clove (optional, for garnish)

Grilled Cheese:

  • Softened Butter
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Garlic Powder

Steps:

Tomato Soup:

  1. Roughly chop onions and fennel bulbs.
  2. Melt butter in pot on medium heat, add onion and fennels, cook till softened.
  3. Add celeery seeds and pepper to tastes.
  4. Stir in flour and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes before flour starts to brown.
  6. Add wine if using, Simmer for atleast 30 minutes, stirring and scraping occassionally.
  7. Puree what is in the pot when ready, add salt, water, sugar and tomatoe paste (if needed) to taste.

Grilled Cheese:

  1. Generously spread softened butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat a pan to medium heat, and toast one slice, butter side down, lay 2-3 slices of cheese, and dust garlic powder (optional) ontop.
  3. Put second slice on top, and cover with lid or foil, browning first side.
  4. Flip and cover, browning the second side.
  5. Optionally flip and cook till sides are dry as desired.
